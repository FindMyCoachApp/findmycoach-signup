Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Find My Coach.

Find My Coach is a platform that connects athletes with certified sports coaches in Kuala Lumpur. We make it easy to find, book, and communicate with coaches for various sports.

Simply browse available coaches in your area, view their profiles and availability, then book a session that fits your schedule. You can also chat with coaches before booking to discuss your goals.

Yes, all coaches on our platform are certified and verified. We check their credentials, experience, and background to ensure quality and safety.

We offer coaching for various sports including tennis, swimming, badminton, football, basketball, and more. New sports are added regularly based on demand.

Payments are processed securely through our platform. You can pay using various methods including credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets. Coaches receive payment after the session is completed.

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule sessions up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Cancellation policies may vary depending on the coach's settings.

To become a coach, you need to apply through our platform, provide your certifications and experience details, and pass our verification process. Join our waitlist to be notified when coach applications open.

We're launching on December 6th, 2025. Join our waitlist to be among the first to experience Find My Coach when we go live!
